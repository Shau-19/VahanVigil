Vahan Vigil

वाहन · vehicle  +  vigil · watchful oversight

Enforcement today is patrol-based and reactive — officers go where they think violations are. Vahan Vigil turns six months of Bengaluru challan data into a live, ranked picture of where illegal parking actually creates congestion pressure, and when each zone needs eyes on it most.

🚓
Why it's hard today
Patrol is reactive — officers respond after congestion forms, with no map of where violations actually hurt traffic flow.
🌓
A blind spot we found
Violation data is enforcement-shaped, not congestion-shaped — most zones show a multi-hour daytime gap with near-zero coverage.
📊
What "priority" really means
High violation count ≠ high priority. Some of our highest-priority zones rank outside the top 20 by raw volume alone.

⚡ Live Scoring API
Live inference is real, not a mockup. The trained Isolation Forest model can score a hypothetical day's stats for any zone on demand via POST /api/score-day — try it at /docs after opening the dashboard.
⚠ What this is, and isn't
Priority scores are structured estimates built from violation severity, vehicle type, road network class, and time-predictability — not a direct traffic-flow sensor reading. They're internally validated (AHP weighting + sensitivity analysis) but not yet checked against observed real-world congestion. Treat this as decision support for where to look first, not a final verdict.
